# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## ✅ Do’s
- Think before you post and consider the long-term impact  
- Use respectful and professional language in all interactions  
- Protect your privacy by limiting personal information shared online  
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation  
- Maintain a positive and responsible digital presence  

## ❌ Don’ts
- Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content  
- Don’t overshare personal or sensitive information  
- Don’t engage in online arguments, trolling, or hate speech  
- Don’t spread fake news or unverified information  
- Don’t post anything that could harm your academic or professional reputation
